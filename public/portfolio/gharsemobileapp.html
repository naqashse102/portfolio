Gharse

On-Demand home services mobile app

GharSe offers a marketplace to help deliver quality home-based covid-protected barber services in Pakistan. It turns home-based barbers into micro-entrepreneurs by connecting them with local clientele.

Gharse is a social enterprise that provides covid-protective, timely, convenient and professional at-home barber services to its clients through a web and mobile based solution. Gharse also provides package deals that meet the demands of its clients.
Gharse vigilant vetting process ensures that we take on board reliable barber professionals. Gharse further up-skills the barbers, who then provide clients high quality, timely and premium berber services in the comfort of their homes.
